Conatural Blue Shampoo

Conatural Blue Shampoo is a specialized hair care product developed to effectively neutralize brass tones in blonde, silver, gray and highlighted hair. Enriched with a unique blend of blue and purple pigments, this shampoo helps counteract unwanted yellow and orange undertones and restores hair to its desired cool, ash or platinum hue. Conatural Blue Shampoo's key ingredient is violet leaf extract, which contains natural pigments that help tone down brassiness and enhance the brightness of blonde and silver hair. These pigments work to neutralize warm tones that can develop over time due to factors such as sun exposure, pollution and impurities in water. In addition to violet leaf extract, this shampoo is enriched with nourishing plant oils such as argan oil and coconut oil to help hydrate and condition hair, leaving it soft, smooth and manageable. These oils also provide antioxidant protection, helping to protect hair from environmental damage and oxidative stress. Conatural Blue Shampoo is free of harsh chemicals such as sulfates, parabens and silicones, making it gentle and safe for everyday use. Its pH-balanced formula ensures that it cleanses hair without stripping it of its natural oils or causing irritation, making it suitable for all hair types, including color-treated hair. To use Conatural Blue Shampoo, simply apply an adequate amount to wet hair, lather and massage into scalp and hair. Leave the shampoo on for 2-3 minutes so that the blue and purple pigments penetrate the hair shaft and neutralize unwanted tones. Rinse thoroughly with water, and then add your favorite conditioner for extra hydration and nourishment. Whether you're a natural blonde, a silver fox or someone with highlighted hair, Conatural Blue Shampoo offers a convenient and effective solution to maintaining your desired hair color. With its gentle yet effective formula, this shampoo helps keep your hair shiny, fresh and salon-worthy, ¬¬¬¬¬¬ensuring you can confidently flaunt your cool-toned hair.
